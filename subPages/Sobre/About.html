GAMIF

GAMIF was a site designed for a web development project.

Gamif is a project designed to expose the works of the Games groups. digital, initially the project would be for the field games network (Exhibiting events and a community), however on recommendation, it became a website to promote the developed games.

Gamif is based on famous layouts like the play store and steam, with a focus on in interactivity, immersiveness
