SOBRE

Epic Games, Inc., formerly called Potomac Computer Systems and Epic MegaGames, is a US-based developer of electronic games and software based in Cary, North Caroline. It was founded in 1991 by Tim Sweeney and was originally located in Potomac City in Maryland.
