ABOUT THE GAME

Resident Evil 7: Biohazard, known in Japan as Biohazard 7: Resident Evil is an electronic game of survival horror genre produced by Resident Evil. by Capcom and released January 24, 2017 for Microsoft Windows PlayStation 4 and Xbox One, with the PlayStation 4 version having full support for PlayStation VR.
