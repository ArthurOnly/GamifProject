ABOUT THE GAME

The Last of Us Remastered for the PS4 system - over 200 Game of the Year awards. The Last of Us is an experience that redefines the genre with a mix of survival and action, telling a story centered on the characters, about a modern plague that decimates the species human 20 years ago. Nature invades civilization, forcing the few survivors to fight for food, weapons and whatever else they can find.

Released Year: 2013
